# **App Name**: BeritaKita

## Core Features:

- Dynamic News Feed Display: Show a personalized and diverse stream of news articles on the homepage, organized by recency or relevance, to provide a unique browsing experience.
- News Article Detail View: Provide a clean, focused reading experience for individual news articles with rich media support (images, videos).
- Content Categorization: Organize articles into thematic categories like 'Politik', 'Ekonomi', 'Olahraga', 'Teknologi' for easy navigation and discovery.
- AI-powered News Rephrasing Tool: Utilize an AI tool to rewrite headlines, summaries, or even sections of news articles from source content, ensuring uniqueness while preserving the original meaning.
- Search and Filter Functionality: Enable users to efficiently search for articles using keywords, and filter results by categories, tags, or publication date.
- Admin Article Management: A simplified backend interface for administrators to submit new articles, categorize them, and manage existing content, before or after AI processing.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: A confident and serene digital blue (#2E7EB8) to evoke trustworthiness and modern information. This color will be used for interactive elements, main headlines, and brand accents.
- Background color: A subtle, desaturated light blue (#F0F3F4) that is visibly distinct from pure white, providing a soft, easy-on-the-eyes backdrop for extensive reading.
- Accent color: A vibrant aqua (#52D5E0) used sparingly to highlight calls to action, user feedback, or key interactive elements, providing a refreshing contrast.
- Headline font: 'Space Grotesk' (sans-serif) for a modern, crisp, and informative header style that reflects a tech-forward approach to news. Body font: 'Inter' (sans-serif) for excellent legibility and a neutral, objective feel for article content.
- Implement a consistent set of minimalist, line-art icons. These icons should be functional for navigation, social sharing, and categorization, maintaining a clean aesthetic.
- Employ a responsive grid-based layout for the news feed, optimizing readability and content presentation across various screen sizes. Articles will feature clear typography, ample whitespace, and prominent imagery.
- Incorporate subtle, tasteful micro-animations, such as smooth transitions for article loading, hover effects on clickable elements, and category filtering, to enhance user interaction without being distracting.
